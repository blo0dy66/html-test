What Is TechCon?

TechCon is a four-day conference focused on technology, design, and innovation. It is meant to bring together designers, innovators, and inspired entrepreneurs from all over the world. TechCon is all about friendly community, challenging ideas, essential insights, and endless inspiration.

During the conference, you'll be able to learn from those thriving in the industry and acquire resources and knowledge to become better in your craft.
